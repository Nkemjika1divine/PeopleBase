About the Project

I embarked on this project because I have seen people exposed to a lot of other people who do not have good intentions at all. So i decided that it could be ok to create an application that allows people to look up other people and know their history. I am not saying that this application is a necessity, neither will I engage in the discussion on the necessity of it. This is just what I think. A lot of people may have different opinion regarding it, especially the fact that people's lives are publicised. I understand that, but however, I decided to make it my portfolio project for Holberton School Here is the github repo containing the project PeopleBase. Check it out.
